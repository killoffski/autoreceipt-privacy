AUTOReceipt — Delete Account & Data

How to Delete Your Data

AUTOReceipt stores all data locally on your device and in your personal OneDrive account. We do not maintain any accounts or store any user data on our servers.

Step 1: Delete Local App Data

  • Open your device Settings → Apps → AUTOReceipt → Storage → Clear Data
  • This removes all local settings, scan history, and cached images
  • Alternatively, uninstall the app to remove all local data

Step 2: Disconnect OneDrive

  • Open AUTOReceipt → Settings → tap "Sign Out"
  • To fully revoke app access: go to Microsoft Account Permissions and remove AUTOReceipt

Step 3: Delete Uploaded Receipts

  • Receipts uploaded to OneDrive are stored in your personal OneDrive account under the Receipts/ folder
  • You can delete these files directly from OneDrive at any time
  • AUTOReceipt has no access to these files after you sign out

What Data is Deleted

  • Local app data: Settings, scan history, cached images — deleted when you clear data or uninstall
  • OneDrive files: Must be deleted manually from your OneDrive account
  • OpenAI processing: Receipt images sent for OCR processing are not stored by our app. Refer to OpenAI's data retention policy
